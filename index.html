What is Sui Bridge?

Sui Bridge is Sui's native bridge for moving supported tokens between Sui and other networks; the official documentation describes supported assets, package IDs, contract addresses, source code, audits, and limits.

How Sui Bridge works

  1. Choose directionOpen the app, select the source network and destination network, then connect the wallets required for that route.
  2. Select an assetPick a supported token, enter the amount, and review the app details before approving anything.
  3. Confirm on sourceApprove the token if required, then sign the source-chain transaction in your wallet.
  4. Track completionFollow the bridge status in the app and verify the destination balance before making another move.

Sui Bridge FAQ

What is Sui Bridge?

Sui Bridge is the native Sui bridge for moving supported assets between Sui and other networks through the official app at bridge.sui.io.

Is Sui Bridge the official app?

Yes, Sui documentation points users to bridge.sui.io for the native bridge experience; always verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

What assets does Sui Bridge support?

Sui docs list WBTC, LBTC, ETH, WETH, and USDT as supported assets, though available routes can vary by direction and app state.

Who operates Sui Bridge?

Sui Bridge is operated and governed by Sui validators, and bridge actions require validator signatures with a threshold of voting power.

How do I verify Sui Bridge contracts?

Use the contract and address links in the official docs, including the Ethereum bridge contract, before relying on copied addresses.
