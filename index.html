Why bridge here

Built for Sui Bridge moves

Sui Bridge is for users who want a direct, wallet-native way to bring assets toward Sui without handing custody to a centralized account.

Non-custodial by design

You connect a wallet, review the route and approve the transaction yourself. The official bridge interface reflects the same self-custody principle: your wallet signs, and the app does not take your private keys.

Made for the Sui route

Bridge from familiar EVM environments such as the Ethereum network, then review the destination, asset and execution path before committing the move.

Live quote visibility

Costs, timing and received amounts can move with network demand, liquidity and route choice. Sui Bridge keeps those details in the confirmation flow so you can decide with the current quote in front of you.

How it works

Bridge to Sui in three steps

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect the wallet holding the asset you want to bridge. A browser wallet such as MetaMask can be used for EVM-side approvals.

2

Choose the route

Select the source chain, destination on Sui and asset. ETH, USDC and supported network routes are shown with live route details inside the app.

3

Review and confirm

Check the quote, expected timing, wallet approvals and network costs, then sign only when the route matches what you want to send.

FAQ

Sui Bridge questions

01

What is Sui Bridge?

Sui Bridge is a non-custodial DeFi app for moving crypto between external networks and the Sui ecosystem. You connect your wallet, choose a route and approve the bridge transaction from your own wallet.

02

Which chains and assets can I use?

The app focuses on Sui routes and common bridge assets such as ETH and native USDC issuer resources. For the live list, use the app or check the supported chains page.

03

How long does a bridge take?

Bridge timing depends on the source network, destination finality, route conditions and wallet confirmation speed. The app shows the current timing estimate before you sign.

04

What does Sui Bridge cost?

You may pay source-chain gas, destination-chain gas or route-specific costs depending on the transaction. Sui Bridge displays the live cost details in the app before confirmation.

05

Is Sui Bridge non-custodial?

Yes. The bridge flow is wallet-first: you keep control of your keys, review approvals and sign the transaction yourself instead of depositing funds into an account.

06

Can I bridge from Base?

If the route is available in the app, you can review the Base ecosystem path, the selected asset and the live quote before you approve anything.
