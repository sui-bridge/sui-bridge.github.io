Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sui Bridge?

Sui Bridge is a leading native water-speed bridge and automated market maker (AMM) built natively on the Sui Network. By utilizing optimized routing scalability, Sui Bridge offers low-fee, near-instant token transfers for prominent assets like BTC, ETH, SOL, AVAX, and LINK. As documented on the CoinMarketCap database, the platform integrates deep pools to prevent transaction slippage while maintaining a non-custodial framework where users retain full ownership of their assets.

How to Bridge Assets on Sui Bridge?

Trading on the platform is straightforward. First, review token contract addresses and current price actions on CoinMarketCap to check market depth. Connect your Web3 wallet, configure the network parameters, and select the token pair you wish to exchange. The routing engine consults liquidity databases on CoinGecko to construct the most cost-efficient path. For a detailed guide on supported networks, check the Supported Chains database.

What are the Security Audits and Gas Optimization on Sui Bridge?

The transactions are registered on the SuiScan. Security protocols conform to rigorous decentralized finance standards as documented by Wikipedia. Sui Bridge dynamically monitors network gas prices to route transfers efficiently, minimizing the overhead cost for every single transaction. Learn more about our audits in the Security Documentation.
